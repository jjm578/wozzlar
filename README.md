# Wozzlar — Daily Phrase Puzzle

Wozzlar is a daily word game where you solve a multi-word phrase one guess at a time. You have **7 guesses** to uncover the full phrase.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open the game — a splash screen shows today's puzzle date and number.
2. Tap **Play** to start.
3. The puzzle appears as a series of blank letter tiles, one group per word in the phrase.

---

## Goal

Solve every word in today's phrase before you run out of guesses.

---

## Basics

- **Tap or click a word** to select it for guessing.
- **Type letters** using your physical keyboard or the on-screen keys.
- **Press ENTER** to submit your guess for the selected word.
- Keep solving words until the entire phrase is complete.

---

## Tile Colors

After you submit a guess, each tile changes colour to give you feedback:

| Colour | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🩷 **Pink** | **HIT** — right letter in the right spot |
| 🟦 **Blue** | **NEAR** — letter is somewhere in the phrase, but not here (tile stays blue until that position is solved) |
| ⬛ **Dark** | **MISS** — letter is not in the phrase at all |

---

## Keyboard Colors

The on-screen keyboard tracks what you've learned:

| Colour | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🩷 **Pink key** | Letter has been placed correctly somewhere in the phrase |
| 🟦 **Blue key** | Letter exists in the phrase but hasn't been placed correctly yet |
| ⬛ **Dark key** | Letter is not in the phrase (the key fades away once confirmed) |
| 🟦 **Blue squares on a key** | Shows how many more of that letter still remain unplaced in the phrase |

---

## Hints Beside Each Word

- The **left side** of each word shows your past unique guesses for that specific word.
- **Underlined letters** in a hint mean that letter belongs in the word but is in a different position.

---

## ALL IN — High-Stakes Mode

- Press the **ALL IN** button to attempt filling in the **entire phrase at once**, instead of word by word.
- **ALL IN counts as one of your 7 guesses.**
- ✅ Correct → you earn the 🏆 **Wozzlar** badge.
- ❌ Incorrect → you earn the 🎺 **Super Womp** badge (even if you go on to finish the puzzle).

---

## Guesses & Badges (Daily Mode)

You get **7 guesses** per day shared across all words in the phrase.

| Result | Badge |
|--------|-------|
| Solve the phrase within 7 guesses | 🏆 **Wozzlar** |
| Need more than 7 guesses | 😐 **Womp** |
| Miss an ALL IN attempt | 🎺 **Super Womp** |

- Win **streaks** accumulate as long as you complete each day's puzzle.
- After 7 guesses with unsolved words remaining you can still keep guessing to finish the puzzle — you just won't improve your badge for the day.

---

## Practice Mode

- Tap the **Practice** button in the top-right corner.
- Play unlimited random puzzles.
- No streaks, no badges — purely for fun and skill-building.

---

## Navigation & Menus

| Element | Action |
|---------|--------|
| ☰ **Hamburger menu** (top-left) | Access **How to Play**, Add to Home Screen, Give Feedback, Contact Us |
| **Wozzlar** brand (top-centre) | View your stats and today's result |
| **Practice** button (top-right) | Switch to practice mode |

---

## Tips

- **Tap any box** in a word to move the cursor to that position.
- **Backspace** deletes letters that haven't been locked in yet.
- Blue tile highlights persist until that position is solved; letters you type appear on top of the highlight so you can still see your ongoing guess.

---

## Daily Reset

A brand-new puzzle appears at **midnight (local time)** every day.

---

## Day / Night Theme

The game automatically switches between a light (day) theme and a dark (night) theme at **6 pm** local time.
