Goal
Solve every word in today’s phrase before you run out of guesses.

Basics
Tap or click a word to work on it.

Type letters using your keyboard or the on-screen keys.

Press ENTER to submit your word.

Keep solving until the full phrase is complete.

Tile Colors
Pink – HIT — right letter, right spot.

Blue – NEAR — letter is in the phrase, but not in this spot. (These stay blue until that position is solved.)

Dark – MISS — letter isn’t in the phrase.

Keyboard Colors
Pink key – letter placed correctly somewhere.

Blue key – letter exists elsewhere in the phrase.

Dark key – letter not in the phrase (the letter will disappear from the key once confirmed).

Pink key with blue squares – shows how many more of that letter remain in the phrase.

Hints Beside Each Word
Left side shows your past unique guesses for that word.

Underlined letters mean the letter belongs in that word but in a different position.

ALL IN (High-Stakes Mode)
This feature allows you to fill in the entire puzzle, not just one word at a time.

ALL IN counts as a guess. If you miss, you earn the 🎺 Super Womp badge.

Get it right, and you earn the 🏆 Wozzlar badge.

Guesses & Badges (Daily)
You get 7 guesses per day.

Solve within the limit → 🏆 Wozzlar badge.

Need more than 7 guesses → 😐 Womp badge.

Miss an ALL IN → 🎺 Super Womp badge.

Win streaks build daily.

Practice Mode
Play unlimited puzzles — no streaks, no badges.

Tips
Tap boxes to move the cursor.

Backspace deletes unlocked letters.

Blue highlights persist until that position is solved; typed letters show on top of the highlight.

Reset
A new daily puzzle appears at midnight (local time).
