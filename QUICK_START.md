# 🚀 Quick Start: Add Your Logo in 3 Simple Steps

Your logo file didn't get added when you dragged it to Codespaces. Here's the easiest way to fix it:

## ✨ The Easiest Way

1. **Find your wozzlar-logo.png file** (512x512 pixels) on your desktop

2. **In Codespaces**, drag it into the **root folder** (where you see README.md, not into any subfolder)

3. **Open Terminal** (Terminal → New Terminal) and run:
   ```bash
   ./add-logo.sh wozzlar-logo.png
   ```

That's it! The script will:
- ✅ Copy it to the right location
- ✅ Stage it for commit
- ✅ Tell you exactly what to do next

- ✅ Logo will appear under "Wozzlar" heading at 180x180px
- ✅ Path is configured: `public/images/wozzlar-logo.png`
- ⏳ Just need you to add the file!
